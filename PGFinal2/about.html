About Tripary

Tripary is your personal travel diary that goes with you anywhere. It features:

  • -View your current location and compass direction.
  • -The ability to record trips and plot points on a map.
  • -Take and save snapshots of your trip.
  • -Take and save video of your trip.
  • -Record journal entries for each trip.

Tipary was developed by Ken Petersen, web and mobile app developer.
